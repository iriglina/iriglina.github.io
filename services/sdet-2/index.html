SDET

As an iOS developer at MX, you would work on the team that owns the development and maintenance of the MX Player Video Streaming app and the MX TakaTak Short Videos app.

Responsibilities

As a developer in our iOS team you would be responsible for the following duties.

  1. Design and code features for the native iOS applications.
  2. Write unit tests for all features.
  3. Ship features in fast moving agile sprints.
  4. Work closely with API, Product Design and Product Management teams to brainstorm and chalk out details of features.

You are a good fit for this role if you

  • are a Software Developer with at least 3 years of experience in building apps for the iOS platform.
  • have Good understanding of Swift.
  • have Good understanding of iOS application architecture and design.
  • have Good understanding of memory management, network interaction, concurrency and multi-threading in iOS.
  • have Good understanding of Foundation and UIKit APIs.
  • have Understanding of complete application development, continuous integration & testing and app distribution infrastructure.

The following would give you an edge over other applicants

  • Good understanding of media capturing, playback, editing and export with AVFoundation and Video Toolbox APIs
  • Past experience of building video streaming apps.
  • Past experience of working with third party libraries and dependency management on iOS.
  • Past experience of developing, testing and maintaining SDKs and libraries.

Why work with us?

  • Opportunity to work on a wide set of Apple devices and platforms providing best features to millions of users.
  • Opportunity to work alongside and learn with our world class iOS engineering team.
  • Opportunity to mentor and guide new engineers as we continue to grow.
  • Opportunity to work on the latest technologies with the best equipment you need.
  • Flexible work hours along with a generous leave policy.
  • Option to work remotely.
  • Competitive compensation and benefits.
  • Stable workplace with a flexible career path.
